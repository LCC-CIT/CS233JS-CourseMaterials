About

Nestled in the heart of Oregon, the Mt. Pisgah Arboretum is a hidden gem that offers a serene and picturesque setting for a delightful picnic. The White Oak Pavilion, with its majestic trees and lush greenery, provides a tranquil backdrop that is sure to make our picnic an unforgettable experience. Imagine sitting under the shade of towering oaks, the gentle rustling of leaves whispering stories of nature, as we savor our picnic treats. The arboretum's diverse flora and fauna add to the charm, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers.

So, mark your calendars and join us for a picnic at the White Oak Pavilion in the Mt. Pisgah Arboretum. Let's come together to enjoy good food, great company, and the beautiful outdoors. Whether you're looking forward to exploring the arboretum's trails, or simply relaxing and soaking in the peaceful ambiance, there's something for everyone. Don't forget to bring your favorite picnic dish to share! Looking forward to seeing you there.
